📘 Mathematics

Mathematics is the study of numbers, shapes, and patterns. It is used in science, engineering, finance, and everyday life.

Key Topics in Math

  • Arithmetic: Basic operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
  • Algebra: Using symbols and letters to solve equations.
  • Geometry: The study of shapes, angles, and measurements.
  • Statistics: The collection and analysis of data.
