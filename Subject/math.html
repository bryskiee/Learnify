Mathematics

Mathematics is the study of numbers, shapes, and patterns. It is essential in everyday life.

  • Arithmetic: Basic operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
  • Algebra: The study of symbols and rules.
  • Geometry: Shapes and their properties.
